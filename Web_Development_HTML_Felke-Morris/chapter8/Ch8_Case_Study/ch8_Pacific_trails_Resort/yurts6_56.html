The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanent structureds four feet off the ground.
Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that
can be opened.
How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and
gas-fired stove. The luxury campoing experience also inclueds
electricity and a sindk with hot and cold running water. Shower and
restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most guests also
pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing
weather with layers of clothing.
