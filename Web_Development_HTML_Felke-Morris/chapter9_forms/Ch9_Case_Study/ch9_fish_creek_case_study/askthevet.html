Question: Our dog, Sparky, likes to eat whatever the kids are snaking on.
Is it OK for the dog to eat chocolate?
Answer: Chocolate is toxic to dogs. Please do not feed you dog chocolate. Try playing a game with your children -- when you feed people treats, they can feed Sparky dog treats.
